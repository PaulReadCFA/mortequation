Mortgage cash flow equations

Monthly Cash Flow Equation

PMT = r 12 × PV 1 - ( 1 + r 12 ) - T

Interest Cash Flow Equation for Year 1

INT 1 = PV × r 12

Interest Cash Flow Equation where t > 1

INT t = ( PV - ∑ i = 1 t - 1 PRN i ) × r 12

Principal Amortization Equation

PRN t = PMT - INT t

Mortgage Amortization:

Early payments are mostly interest; the principal portion of each payment increases over time as the outstanding balance decreases.
